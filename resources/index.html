Silver Age Minecraft Key Links

  • MultiMC Launcher – An excellent 3rd party launcher, capable of supporting various mods and other settings with ease.
  • Betacraft Launcher – A custom launcher designed for legacy Minecraft versions.
  • Betacraft Proxy – Enables skin and session support for older versions.
